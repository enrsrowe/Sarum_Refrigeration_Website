About Us

Our aim is to provide you with the facilities you need, and the service you expect, all at an affordable cost.

Sarum Refrigeration Services Ltd has been established for over 50 years and are well positioned in the marketplace to provide comprehensive sales and service support for all air conditioning, refrigeration, ventilation and extraction projects. We have a team of fully qualified engineers who have a wealth of knowledge and experience in all aspects of mechanical engineering matters.

Operating across the whole of the South of England, Sarum Refrigeration Services Ltd offers the total solution, from the initial design and installation, through to final commissioning.

Why Us?

As members of HVCA Association, with engineers trained to NVQ Level 3 in Refrigeration Practice, Sarum Refrigeration Services Ltd offers a highly professional service for all clients, big and small.

Based in Salisbury Wiltshire we are ideally placed to service the whole of the South of England, and can quickly respond to clients when the need arises.
