Services


Operating across the whole of the South of England, Sarum Refrigeration Services Ltd offers the total solution, from the initial design and installation, through to final commissioning.



Design

For refurbishment and new-build projects, our team of surveyors and engineers should be involved from the very early stages in order to ensure that the installation is correctly planned, and all eventualities are catered for.

Whether you are refurbishing existing premises, or involved in a new-build, our surveyors need to be involved right from the start. This not only ensures that your architects and other contractors have access to the latest options and technologies, but also to make sure that they take account of the necessary considerations for the installation of an effective and efficient system.

By liaising with the rest of the project team, our surveyors help to eliminate the need for last-minute structural alterations, that can prove to be so costly.

By involving us from the start, you can not only ensure that you have the best bespoke system for your needs, but also that it is being provided at the best cost.

Installation

Whether you are purchasing a small stand-alone cooler, or complete extraction system for your factory, our engineers take the same care and attention to ensure that the product is installed and functioning to your specifications.

Correct installation is paramount to ensuring an efficient and economical system. Whether it is a stand-alone wine chiller in a bar or restaurant, a walk-in cold room at a hotel, or a full ventilation system for a office complex, our trained engineers ensure that the equipment is installed to the highest standards, and fully tested to ensure your satisfaction.

Once installation has been finalised, full training will be given in the use of the equipment, and we will discuss maintenance options to ensure the continued smooth running.
Our staff are always on hand to answer any queries you may have during initial use of your new system.

Supply

Should you already know what you require, or you simply need stand-alone units, we can supply from a wide range of manufacturers to meet your needs.

Should you require stand-alone equipment, we are happy to discuss your requirements, and supply from a wide-range of manufacturers, according to your needs.

Equipment can range from counter-top wine coolers, to portable air-conditioning units for home or small office. Whatever you need, call us and we will provide appropriate equipment at competitive prices.

Maintenance

Whether your system was installed by us, or someone else, correct maintenance is essential to ensure smooth and economical running.

However well a system is installed, it will require regular maintenance to ensure that it continues to run smoothly and efficiently.

Once installation has been finalised, full training will be given in the use of the equipment, and we will discuss maintenance options to ensure the continued smooth running.

Maintenance options for equipment installed by us will be discussed at the time. However our engineers are also happy to assess existing installations and arrive at an appropriate maintenance plan.
